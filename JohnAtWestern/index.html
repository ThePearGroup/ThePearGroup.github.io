Diversity.

Like any BCPS school west of Baltimore, Western Tech is very diverse, incorperating students of all kinds of ethnic backrounds and religeons, incorperating all regardless of gender.

I, John Cummiskey have found myself in great relife at the diversity found at Western Tech. Coming from Southwest Accadamy, a very diverse middle school with students of many races and religeons, having also indluded many students LGBTQ+ community, I am one to greatly appreicate diverserity and see its great benifits to society. From this, I greatly enjoy diversity in schools.

How is it for John Cummiskey?

I have (of course) have found great acceptence at Western Tech, with my lack of religeon as a cis white male never posing any issue.
